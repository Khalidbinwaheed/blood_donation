Lifeline Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information we collect

Lifeline collects account information (name, email, blood group), location when you request emergency or donation services, health vitals you choose to record, and messages you send through the app.

How we use information

We use your data to match blood donors and recipients, coordinate appointments, show nearby centers, deliver notifications, and provide the in-app health helper.

Sharing

Blood request details may be visible to authenticated donors in your area. We do not sell personal data. Third-party services (Firebase, Google, GitHub Models for AI chat) process data under their own policies.

Security

Data is stored in Firebase with role-based access rules. AI chat requests are processed server-side; API keys are never embedded in the mobile or web app.

Your choices

You can update notification preferences and blood group in Settings, or delete your account by contacting support.

Contact

For privacy questions, email your Lifeline administrator or project maintainer.
